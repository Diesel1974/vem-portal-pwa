Add the Client Portal to your home screen
One tap to your portal — no login screen to bookmark, no app store needed.
Mobile
Desktop

iPhone · Safari

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari (square with an up arrow).
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add in the top right corner.

Android · Chrome

  1. Tap the Install VEM Portal button above. (If you don't see it, tap the menu ⋮ in Chrome and choose Install app.)
  2. Tap Install in the popup.

The VEM icon will appear on your home screen. Tap it any time to open your portal.

Desktop · Chrome or Edge

  1. Look for the install icon in your address bar (small monitor with a down arrow, on the right side).
  2. Click it, then click Install.
  3. The VEM Portal opens in its own window. Pin it to your taskbar or dock for one-click access.
